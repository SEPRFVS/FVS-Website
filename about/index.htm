The Story of Trains across Europe

Back in October 2014, Trains across Europe (TaxE) was proposed by the University of York as the SEPR project for 2014/15. This concept was developed so that in January a first version of it was released. During the compulsory swap stage it was taken on by 6 teams to be developed further.

We took on a different project, one designed by ADB, to develop further. This was extended to be a full implementation of the game and was made available at the end of the third assessment.

Then we took on the game designed in the previous assessment made by DUS, which is based off our assessment 2 submission. This was then extended to include the new requirements (connection modification and replay) and is now available in a finnished form.
